About TGirl.click

What is Tgirl.click? TGirl.click is a link shortener / redirect service that makes it more (or less) likely that your friends will click on your links.
How can I create vanity links?
Vanity links with specific names are not a publicly accessible feature at this time. If you really want one, shoot me a message at vanilla@nya.gg.
